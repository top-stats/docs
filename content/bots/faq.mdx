<Accordions type='single'>
  <Accordion id='what-is-top-stats' title='What is TopStats?'>
    TopStats, previously DBL Statistics, is a analytical site that provides statistics on your favourite Discord Bots
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion id='where-does-top-stats-get-its-data' title='Where does TopStats get its data?'>
    We source our Discord bot data from Top.gg, every hour.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion id='remove-wrong-data' title='I posted the wrong data to Top.gg and its shown on your site, can I remove this data point?'>
    If you would like to remove a certain data point in your data, you can do so by contacting us in our [Discord Server](https://discord.gg/VDf8CG6KGA).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion id='why-not-showing' title='Why is my bot not showing up?'>
    This may be because your bot has not yet been approved, or has recently been approved. Please wait till the next hour for it to pop up.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion id='data-incorrect' title='Why is my bots data incorrect or not showing up?'>
    In order for your data to appear on TopStats, you will need to post your server count and shard count to [Top.gg](https://top.gg) through their API.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion id='data-issue' title='There is an issue with my data or a graph isnt showing what Id expect.'>
    Please contact our team on our [Discord Server](https://discord.gg/VDf8CG6KGA), so that we can assist you with this.
  </Accordion>
  <section id="request-data">
  <Accordion id='request-data' title='Can I request all my data?'>
    At this moment we don't have a way for you to do this. We may in the future but until then unfortunately there is no way to do this.
    However, you can request access to or deletion of your personal data by contacting us via email at privacy@topstats.gg
  </Accordion>
  </section>
  <section id="delete-data">
  <Accordion id='delete-data' title='How can I delete all of my data?'>
    Please contact our team at privacy@topstats.gg, and we will remove your personal data. Please note that if you log back into our site after account deletion, we will store your data once more
  </Accordion>
  </section>
  <Accordion id='affiliation' title='Are we affiliated with Top.gg?'>
    No, TopStats is an independent entity and we are not affiliated with [Top.gg](https://top.gg).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion id='change-time-format' title='Can I change the time format?'>
    TopStats always adjusts the time format to the language set in your browser. Change the language in your browser to change the time format.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
