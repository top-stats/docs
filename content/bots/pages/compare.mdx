## Compare Multiple Bots

Select up to 4 bots you want to compare with each other - with Premium, you can even compare up to 8!
![get-bot-image](/content/bots/compare-page/select-bots.png)

---

Then click on "Compare" to confirm your selection.
![compare-bots](/content/bots/compare-page/compare-bots.png)

---

Each bot has its own color. You can use these colors to tell the bots apart!
![compare-page](/content/bots/compare-page/compare-page.png)
