The [Leaderboard page](https://topstats.gg/discord/leaderboard) on TopStats.gg displays the top Discord bots based on various metrics such as votes, server count, and more.

---

## Sort by category

You can sort the leaderboard by a specific category by clicking on the respective title.
![Leaderboard Page](/content/bots/leaderboard/sort-monthly-votes.png)

---

## Trends

For each bot, you can see how many positions it has gained or lost on the current day.
![Leaderboard Trends](/content/bots/leaderboard/trends.svg)

---

## Compare bots

You can compare multiple bots by selecting them using the checkboxes next to their names. Then click the “Compare” button displayed at the bottom of the ranking list.
![Compare Bots](/content/bots/leaderboard/leaderboard-compare.png)

![Sonner](/content/bots/leaderboard/sonner.svg)

<Callout type='info'>
  You can compare up to 4 bots at once — with Premium, that limit jumps to 8 ✨
</Callout>

---

## Change the page

If there are more bots than fit on one page, you can navigate through the leaderboard using the pagination buttons at the bottom.
![pagination](/content/bots/leaderboard/pagination.svg)
