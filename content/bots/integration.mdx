You can analyze directly on topstats which of your users voted the most on Top.gg!

<Callout>
  This feature requires an [approved
  bot](https://support.top.gg/hc/en-us/articles/23135162935708-How-to-Add-Your-Bot)
  on top.gg!
</Callout>

<Steps>
<Step>

### Authorize TopStats as Third-party Application

Go to `https://top.gg/bot/:id/dashboard/integrations` and click on **Integrations & API** in the sidebar. Then search for **TopStats** and click on **Install**.

![step-1](/content/bots/integration/step-1.png)

</Step>

<Step>

TopStats should now have been added as a third-party application.

![step-2](/content/bots/integration/integration.png)

</Step>

<Step>

Visit `https://topstats.gg/discord/bots/:id/leaderboard` to check if it works

![leaderboard](/content/bots/integration/leaderboard.png)

</Step>

</Steps>
