## FAQ
<Accordions type="single">
  <Accordion id='refund-policy' title="What is your refund policy?">
    We will process refund requests up to 14 days after purchase. Your refund will be sent to the payment method used.
    Refunds will be pro-rated, meaning you will be refunded a portion of your payment, eqvivalent to the amount of time that hadn't yet elapsed.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion id='apply-refund' title="How do I apply for a refund?">
    To apply for a refund, please send an email to refunds@topstats.gg, with your TopStats ID (which you can find in
    settings > profile).
    <Steps>
      <Step>
        ### Open the settings [!toc]
        Click on the settings icon on the [home page](https://topstats.gg).
        ![settings-image](/content/faq/settings/settings.png)
      </Step>
      <Step>
        ### Open the profile settings [!toc]
        Click on the "Profile" tab in the settings menu.
        ![profile-image](/content/faq/settings/profile.png)
      </Step>
      <Step>
        ### Find your TopStats ID [!toc]
        Your TopStats ID is located under the "Account Information" section.
        ![topstats-id-image](/content/faq/settings/copy-id.png)
      </Step>
      <Step>
        ### Send an email [!toc]
        Send an email to refunds@topstats.gg with your TopStats ID and reason for the refund.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion id='payment-methods' title="What payment methods do you accept?">
    We accept the following payment methods:
    - **All credit & debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)**
    - **PayPal**
    - **Apple Pay**
    - **Google Pay**
    - **Link**
    - **iDEAL**

    *Don't see a payment method, you would like us to support? Reach out to us!*
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion id='edit-premium' title="Can I change or cancel my premium plan?">
    You can modify or cancel your plan at any time. If you cancel your plan, it will be cancelled from the next billing
    date onwards. You can do this by going to Settings > Profile > Cancel.
    <Steps>
      <Step>
        ### Open the settings [!toc]
        Click on the settings icon on the [home page](https://topstats.gg).
        ![settings-image](/content/faq/settings/settings.png)
      </Step>
      <Step>
        ### Open the premium settings [!toc]
        Click on the "Premium" tab in the settings menu.
        ![premium-image](/content/faq/settings/premium.png)
      </Step>
      <Step>
        ### Edit your plan [!toc]
        From here, you can modify or cancel your premium plan.
        ![modify-cancel-image](/content/faq/settings/edit_premium.png)
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
